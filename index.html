WattWise

 Your energy-awareness companion, blending AI technology and personalized advice to help you visualize the total energy you use in a day across food, transport and the home. By combining the data available from these topics and your own input on your daily habits, Wattwise offers real-time insights to build awareness of what your energy usage truly is, and offers advice to make small changes that have a big collective impact. Wattwise aims to empower individuals to become aware of and embrace regenerative practices without sacrificing quality of life. Its purpose is making sustainability accessible and achievable for all.

What Have We Been Up To

Core Values

Sustainable Living

We aim to design solutions that enable people to shift habits to more sustainable choices.

Knowledge Empowerment

Through open-sourced design we design for the growth of knowledge and stimulating people to learn more about current problems.

Energy Awareness

We want people to understand what energy is and where it comes from, so that they can better understand their impact on the planet.

Shifting peoples energy habits to more sustainable behaviour through humanlike interaction, bridging the gap from data to understanding.
